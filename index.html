Projects

Analyzing Amazon's Bestsellers:
Decoding Literary Trends (2009-2022)

In this project, I embarked on an analytical journey through Amazon's best-selling books from 2009 to 2022. Utilizing a dataset sourced from Kaggle, I explored the intricate patterns and trends that define readers' preferences over this period. The analysis covered diverse dimensions, from genre distribution and user rating trajectories to the volume of reviews and pricing strategies of bestsellers. Key visualizations, such as pie charts, line graphs, heatmaps, and stacked bar charts, were employed to make the data both comprehensible and captivating. The insights derived offer a deep understanding of the literary market's dynamics, providing valuable cues for publishers, authors, and literary enthusiasts. Through this exploration, I aimed to showcase the power of data in decoding the evolving tastes and preferences of a global readership.

Decoding the Rhythms: A Decade of Spotify's Top 100

In the digital streaming era, platforms such as Spotify have transformed how music is consumed, showcasing the evolution of global musical tastes and trends. "Decoding the Rhythms" delves into Spotify's Top 100 songs from 2010 to 2019, illustrating a decade enriched by musical innovations and changing listener inclinations. This analysis offers an in-depth view of song attributes, genres, and popularity metrics, presenting essential insights beneficial to music aficionados, industry insiders, and data enthusiasts. The project seeks to decode the sophisticated blend of sounds and rhythms that engaged global audiences and epitomized a decade of music on Spotify.
